Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how bl0btoshii (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information in connection with the animated short-form video content (the “Content”) we publish on third-party social media platforms, including but not limited to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram (collectively, the “Platforms”).

By accessing, viewing, or interacting with our Content on any Platform, you (“you”, “your”, “user”, “viewer”) acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

bl0btoshii is a content creator that produces and publishes original animated short-form videos on third-party Platforms. We do NOT operate a standalone website, mobile application, or any service that directly collects personal information from users.

Key points:

  • We do not directly collect, store, process, or sell your personal information
  • We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any direct tracking technologies
  • We do not require you to create an account with us
  • We do not collect email addresses, names, payment information, or any other personal data from viewers
  • All interactions with our Content occur within third-party Platforms that have their own privacy policies

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do NOT do:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information through our Content
  • We do not collect your IP address, device information, or browser information
  • We do not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not collect location data, demographic data, or behavioral data
  • We do not collect payment or financial information
  • We do not collect information about children or minors beyond what is provided by the Platforms’ own systems
  • We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade any personal information to third parties

3. Third-Party Platform Analytics

3.1 What Platform Analytics We Access

The Platforms on which we publish Content provide us with aggregated, anonymized analytics data through their built-in creator tools and APIs. This data is collected by the Platforms themselves — not by us — and is made available to us as a content creator. This may include:

  • View counts: Total number of views on our videos
  • Engagement metrics: Number of likes, comments, shares, and saves (aggregated)
  • Audience demographics: Aggregated, anonymized data such as general age ranges, gender distribution, and geographic regions of our audience (provided by the Platforms in aggregate form only)
  • Watch time: Average and total watch time on our videos (aggregated)
  • Completion rate: Percentage of viewers who watch our videos to completion (aggregated)
  • Traffic sources: How viewers found our Content (e.g., search, For You page, direct) (aggregated)

3.2 How We Use Platform Analytics

We use the aggregated analytics data described above solely for the following purposes:

  • To understand which Content resonates with our audience
  • To improve the quality and relevance of future Content
  • To make internal content strategy decisions (e.g., which characters or topics to feature)
  • To generate internal performance reports

3.3 Important Distinction

The analytics data we access is collected and controlled by the Platforms, not by us. We access only what the Platforms provide to content creators through their standard creator dashboards and APIs. We cannot identify individual users from this aggregated data.

  • TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, data collection, or data processing of any third-party Platform. Any personal information you provide to these Platforms (such as your account information, comments, likes, or messages) is subject to their privacy policies, not ours.

5. Comments and Public Interactions

If you comment on, like, share, or otherwise publicly interact with our Content on any Platform:

  • That interaction is governed by the Platform’s privacy policy, not ours
  • Your comment, username, and profile picture may be publicly visible to other users of that Platform
  • We may read and respond to public comments but we do not store or process this information outside of the Platform
  • We do not use comments or interaction data for any purpose beyond responding to our audience within the Platform’s native tools

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Content is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly target, market to, or collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

6.3 Platform Responsibilities

The Platforms on which we publish Content have their own policies and mechanisms for protecting children’s privacy, including age-gating and restricted modes. We encourage parents and guardians to review the privacy policies and parental controls of any Platform their children use.

6.4 Parental Concerns

7. Data Security

For our internal operations (such as API credentials and analytics data), we employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures, including:

  • Secure storage of API credentials using environment variables (not hardcoded)
  • Access controls limiting who can access internal systems
  • Regular review of security practices
